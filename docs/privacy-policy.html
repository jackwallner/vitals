Vitals Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Overview

Vitals is a personal health tracking app that displays your daily calories burned and step count. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app is built.

Data Collection

Vitals does not collect, transmit, or share any of your data.

  • No data is sent to any server
  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No third-party SDKs or frameworks
  • No advertising
  • No account creation required

HealthKit Data

Vitals requests read-only access to the following HealthKit data types:

  • Active Energy Burned (calories)
  • Basal Energy Burned (resting calories)
  • Step Count

This data is read from Apple Health and displayed within the app. Vitals never writes to or modifies your health records. HealthKit data is processed entirely on your device and is used solely to display your health metrics and track progress toward your goals.

Your health data is never used for advertising, marketing, data mining, or any purpose other than improving your personal health management within the app. Your health data is never shared with third parties.

You can revoke Vitals' access to your health data at any time by going to Settings > Health > Data Access & Devices > Vitals and turning off all permissions.

Local Storage

Vitals stores a local cache of your daily health summaries on your device using SwiftData. This cache enables widgets and complications to display your data. This data never leaves your device.

Your preferences (goals, display settings, appearance) are stored locally in UserDefaults on your device.

Data Export

Vitals offers a CSV export feature for your historical data. When you export, the file is generated locally on your device and shared only through the method you choose (AirDrop, email, etc.). Vitals does not retain a copy of exported files. Exported files contain sensitive health information — please share them securely.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data remains on your device until you delete the app. Deleting Vitals removes all locally stored data. No data persists on any external server because no data is ever transmitted externally.

To delete your data, you can either uninstall the app (which removes all stored data) or revoke HealthKit access in Settings to stop all data reading. No additional request is needed since no data exists outside your device.

Children's Privacy

Vitals does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect any information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
