Vitals Support

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Vitals is a personal health tracker for iPhone and Apple Watch that displays your daily calories burned and step count using Apple Health data.

Support contact
Email: jackwallner@gmail.com

What Vitals Needs

Vitals requires read-only Apple Health access to:

  • Active Energy Burned
  • Basal Energy Burned
  • Step Count

Troubleshooting

  • If Vitals shows no data, open the Health app and confirm Vitals has permission to read calories and steps.
  • If widgets or complications look stale, open Vitals once to refresh the local cache.
  • If export fails, try again and make sure the destination app or share target is available.

Privacy

Vitals does not create an account, does not send your data to a server, and does not include analytics, ads, or tracking.

Read the full Privacy Policy.

Additional Help
